3D CAMERA CALIBRATION (geometry and 3D reconstruction)

A project developed by Stefano Bonetto and Simone Roman

Welcome to our 3D Camera Calibration demo! This project provides insights into how multiple cameras perceive the same 3D space for applications in computer vision and robotics.

We start with intrinsic camera calibration to identify each camera's internal characteristics, such as focal length and distortion, using calibration patterns from various angles.

Then, we proceed to extrinsic camera calibration, determining the spatial relationships between the cameras to create a cohesive 3D representation of the environment.

Our interactive tool allows you to select a point in the field, instantly visualizing it across all camera views, showcasing the differences in perspective.

Additionally, we offer 3D ball tracking, which detects and tracks a moving ball across multiple camera views, providing real-time tracking useful for sports analysis and robotic navigation.

Discover how our project enhances your understanding of spatial data!
